This is an A4 size book of poetry based on the issue of alcoholism. These poems were written to share and express the feelings and emotions that are felt when a friend, partner or loved one is consumed and dependent on alcohol. This was a cathartic process for me, looking at different poetry styles and writing from differing perspectives. Included are blank pages for the reader to respond to the poems with their thoughts or to create their own poems using their own experiences.
