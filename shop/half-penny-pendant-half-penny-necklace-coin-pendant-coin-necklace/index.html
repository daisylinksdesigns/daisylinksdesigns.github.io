After teaching students about SRE I was inspired to create a necklace as a gentle and cheeky reminder about sexual health. I have kept with my retro theme and chose to incorporate the demonetised half penny coin (which was withdrawn from circulation in the UK in December 1984).

‘Keep your hand on your half penny’ used to be a saying that Mothers said to their Daughters before they want out on a date. This was because the words ‘half penny’ was used as a euphorism for the female sexual organ.

You will also get a copy of the music hall song which did the rounds in the Victorian era, as well as being sung up to at least the 70's by folk singers.
